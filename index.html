Soundtrack of My Life

My name is Emily and I love listening to music. Spotify is likely my most frequently used app. If I were to compile a playlist of my favorite songs and songs that evoke certain memories and experiences of my life, this would be it.

Playlist:

1. Paris in the Rain by Lauv

Lauv is my favorite artist of all time. All of his songs could make this list. I put Paris in the Rain because it was the first song Iâ€™ve ever heard by Lauv. Paris in the Rain evokes memories of my older brother for me, because we both love Lauv's music.

Listen to Paris in the Rain!

2. Love and Run by Lexie Liu

I love that this song is by a woman of color that I can look up to.

Listen to Love and Run!

3. I Want It That Way by Backstreet Boys

This song means a lot to me because it reminds me of my dad, who played this around the house growing up. I get a feeling of home whenever I listen to I Want It That Way. It is also my go-to karaoke song!

Listen to I Want It That Way!

4. Sunday Morning by Matoma

I love EDM, so songs by Matoma, San Holo, Porter Robinson, Gryffin, and ILLENIUM perfectly emcompass this portion of my music taste.

Listen to Sunday Morning!

5. Burn from Hamilton

I absolutely love every song on the Hamilton soundtrack. I chose Burn because it's such a powerful and angry song. I got to see Hamilton with one of my best friends, so this is a good memory for me.

Listen to Burn!

6. Cruise - Remix by Florida Georgia Line

Country music is my guilty pleasure. I listen to this song whenever my friends and I are driving down the highway, so it hypes us up. Cruise just reminds me of all the fun trips that I get to go with my best friends, and how amazing and precious our friendship is.

Listen to Cruise!

7. So Sick by Ne-Yo

The perfect sad song to listen to on a slow day.

Listen to So Sick!

8. Passionfruit by Drake

Because I miss old Drake.

Listen to Passionfruit!

9. Run the World (Girls) by Beyonce

Because I'm all about female empowerment, and the strongest people I know are women, such as my mom.

Listen to Run the World (Girls)!

10. So Long (feat. Cam) by Diplo

So Long is the perfect balance between EDM and country

Listen to So Long!

11. Move, Shake, Drop by Dj Laz

The one song that will always get me dancing

Listen to Move, Shake, Drop!

12. Act Up by City Girls

I love rap in general, so Act Up is only one of the many songs in my rap playlist.

Listen to Act Up!

13. I'll Make a Man Out of You from Mulan

I'll Make a Man Out of You is my favorite Disney song from my favorite Disney movie. I've always loved Disney movies growing up. Mulan was the first time I saw someone who looked like me on film, so it was empowering to see someone like her on TV.

Listen to I'llMake a Man Out of You!
